Sustainable Consumption & Production

Follow the journey to shape the world to be a better place

The UN SDG goal 12 focuses on:

  • Making more use of sustainability in production industry
  • the responsible reduction of food waste, chemical waste
  • raising awareness on the sustainable practices

The values of this goal:

This goal aims to move towards sustainable economic growth, while reducing the negative effects of production and consumption on the environment. It includes promoting resource efficiency as well.

Why?

While populations are continuing to grow, the planet will not have sufficient resources to support people current lifestyles. The environmental degradation endangers the sustainable system on which our future development and very survival depend.

Real-World Examples

IKEA has committed to becoming a fully circular and climate-positive business by 2030. This includes:

  • Designing all products with new circular principles
  • Using only renewable and recycled materials
  • Implementing programs to encourage customers to repair, reuse, and recycle products
